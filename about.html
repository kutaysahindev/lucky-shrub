ABOUT US

Welcome to Lucky Shrub, a medium-sized garden design firm specializing in garden design, creation, maintenance, and landscaping. Nestled in the scenic landscape of Taos, New Mexico, Lucky Shrub also operates as a small plant nursery, offering a comprehensive solution for clients looking to "transform any space into an oasis they can be proud of."

Lucky Shrub was founded by a passionate husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a deep-rooted love for plants. Jason, the garden architect, is the creative force behind all our designs and oversees our dedicated team of landscapers. Meanwhile, Maria leads the company's marketing efforts and manages the nursery, ensuring our clients have access to a wide array of indoor and outdoor plants.
